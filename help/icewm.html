What is IceWM?

IceWM is the lightweight and user-friendly window manager used in Swift Linux. It is also the default window manager of antiX Linux, one of the distros that inspired Swift Linux. Please note that IceWM provides only the menu and the toolbar at the bottom of the screen. The desktop icons and background are provided by ROX pinboard.

How do I change the clock display in the lower right corner of the screen?

Home icon on your desktop -> View -> Show Hidden. Then go to the IceWM directory, open the preferences file, search for "TimeFormat", and change the time format. To show a 12-hour display instead of a 24-hour display, change that line to read TimeFormat="%H:%M" .

Why aren't my IceWM changes showing up?

You may need to restart IceWM. You can do this by going to Main menu -> Logout -> Restart Icewm .
