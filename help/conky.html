Swift Linux Help: Conky

What is Conky?

Conky is the system monitoring display that you see at the bottom of your Swift Linux desktop.

Where is the Conky configuration file?

Click on the Home icon on your desktop and go to View -> Show Hidden and then open the .conkyrc file with Geany.
