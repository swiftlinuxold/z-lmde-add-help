LightDM Login/Display Manager

How do I change the wallpaper for the login screen?

Go to Control Center -> Wallpaper Wizard -> Login Wallpaper Wizard .

How do I enable auto-login?

  1. As root, open the file /etc/lightdm/lightdm.conf and take note of the lines that read: 
    	# autologin-user=mint
	# autologin-user-timeout=0
	# autologin-session=lightdm-autologin
  2. Remove the "#" symbols from the above lines. Replace "mint" with your username. Save the file.
  3. The next time you boot up, you will be logged in automatically.
  4. To disable auto-login again, add the "#" symbol to the beginning of all lines beginning with "autologin".
